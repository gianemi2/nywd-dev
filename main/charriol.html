Why Choose Charriol Eyewear?

Charriol eyewear collections feature a variety of styles, including aviator, cat eye, wayfarer, and rectangular frames. The frames are made from high-quality materials such as stainless steel, titanium, and 18-karat gold. They are particularly famous for their iconic cable-style frames which are made exclusively in France. This style features strands of stainless steel interwoven through each temple. Charriol sunglasses are equipped with state-of-the-art lens technology that provides 100% UV protection and reduced glare. The lenses are also scratch-resistant and treated with an anti-reflective coating.
